Welcome to Light Green

Light and Green Ltd is a healthy and environmental friendly restaurant offering low carbohydrate foods, green color focused in an unconventional way but still ensuring to provide sufficient nutrients for gourmets. All foods are well cooked and served with clear labels about nutrition information. .

In response to the market need for those who are striving to reach healthy diet in order to improve their health condition and lifestyle within their budget, Light and Green is born for close the gap. Light and Green is passionate about bringing healthy lifestyle to everyone in the world because it fully acknowledges the significance of health as the precious asset of each human being. Because it strongly believes and comprehends that positive physical healthy takes such great momentum in mental, spiritual and emotional health. Light and Green endeavours to start from the basic needs of this process with the expectation to bring awareness to everyone, to change their eating habit and to make themselves healthier, happier

Human resources is the core of business operation which employing seasoned management or constantly striving for mentoring would be an insured ticket for further development of the company
