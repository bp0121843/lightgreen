About us

Light and Green is a healthy restaurant chain serving healthy, environmental friendly, organic food for those who have paid serious attention to health condition through daily lifestyle and food consumption. The location is in Wood Green - one of the metropolitan centres in Greater London area with various options for shopping and restaurants. The uniqueness of the business is to get rid of sugar and high carbohydrate in its ingredients that still assure to supply sufficient nutrients, protein, fiber and energy for gourmets. Health is often considered as a significant factor in food choice leading to the multitude of health targeted food products in the market

Light and Green is the place to serve its customers with the most fresh, authentic, healthy ingredients prepared in the lightest way with full of nutrition and energy under the most affordable price.

The company product has features such as: The unique concept of low carbohydrate, no sugar menu introduction

All ingredients are organic and ensured to be selected carefully from Wattson & Patts as well as Clearspring Ltd. About cooking process, all dishes are prepared with less salt, no sugar and healthy olive oil for all frying or stirring ones. Sugar reduction is highly recommended in the food and beverage industry through producing smaller portions for consumption as well as labelling of free sugars in its products

The restaurant proposes to create its first establishment in the short term and strives for expanding in the long term while the populace is more conscious about healthy food and willing to pay for their healthy food consumption. This paper will detail on the first opening, which is located in Wood Green - one of the metropolitan centres in Greater London. The business targets to lure attention and purchase decisions from citizens in the area who are highly health conscious, prefer light meals to the daily heavy food consumption.
