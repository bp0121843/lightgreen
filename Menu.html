Light and Green is the place to serve its customers with the most fresh, authentic, healthy ingredients prepared in the lightest way with full of nutrition and energy under the most affordable price. To grow as the one of the most dominant brands about health conscious in food industry - the less we consume, the more we protect the environment.

In response to the market need for those who are striving to reach healthy diet in order to improve their health condition and lifestyle within their budget, Light and Green is born for close the gap. Light and Green is passionate about bringing healthy lifestyle to everyone in the world because it fully acknowledges the significance of health as the precious asset of each human being. Because it strongly believes and comprehends that positive physical healthy takes such great momentum in mental, spiritual and emotional health.

Light and Green endeavours to start from the basic needs of this process with the expectation to bring awareness to everyone, to change their eating habit and to make themselves healthier, happier through their daily activities - specifically here is their everyday regime
